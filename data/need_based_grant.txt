# Horizon Need-Based Grant

## Overview
The Horizon Need-Based Grant is established to support students from low-income families, ensuring that financial constraints do not hinder their educational pursuits.

## Eligibility Criteria
- **Financial Need:** Must demonstrate significant financial need as determined by the FAFSA or the university's financial aid office. Maximum family income should not exceed $45,000 annually.
- **Minimum GPA:** 6.5 on a 10.0 scale.
- **Academic Standing:** Must be enrolled at least half-time (6+ credits).
- **Residency:** Must be a legal resident or citizen of the country.

## Award Amount
- Variable, up to $8,000 per academic year based on the level of financial need.
- Funds are directly applied to tuition, fees, and on-campus housing.

## Application Procedure
1. Complete and submit the FAFSA by March 15th.
2. Submit the Horizon Grant supplementary form available on the university portal.
3. Provide latest tax returns or proof of income for the household.

## Deadline
March 15th for the upcoming Fall semester.
