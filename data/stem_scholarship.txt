# Future Innovators STEM Scholarship

## Overview
Aimed at encouraging students, particularly those from underrepresented groups, to pursue careers in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM).

## Eligibility Criteria
- **Major:** Must be declared in a STEM field (Computer Science, Engineering, Mathematics, Physics, Biology, Chemistry, etc.).
- **Minimum GPA:** 8.0 on a 10.0 scale.
- **Academic Standing:** Sophomores, Juniors, and Seniors only.
- **Extracurriculars:** Demonstrated involvement in STEM-related clubs, research, or personal projects is highly preferred.

## Award Amount
- $5,000 per academic year.
- Includes a guaranteed interview for the summer research internship program at the university's innovation lab.

## Application Procedure
1. Apply via the standard university scholarship portal.
2. Submit a one-page project proposal or a portfolio of past technical projects (e.g., GitHub link, research paper, lab report).
3. One letter of recommendation from a STEM faculty member.

## Deadline
June 30th.
