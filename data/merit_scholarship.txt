# Global Excellence Merit Scholarship

## Overview
The Global Excellence Merit Scholarship is designed to recognize and reward outstanding academic achievement among undergraduate students.

## Eligibility Criteria
- **Minimum GPA:** 9.5 on a 10.0 scale.
- **Academic Standing:** Must be a full-time undergraduate student.
- **Major:** Open to all majors.
- **Residency:** Open to both domestic and international students.
- **Disciplinary Record:** Must have a clean disciplinary record with the university.

## Award Amount
- $10,000 per academic year, renewable for up to four years provided the student maintains a 9.5 GPA and full-time status.

## Application Procedure
1. Submit the online scholarship application form by May 1st.
2. Provide two letters of recommendation from academic professors.
3. Submit a 500-word personal statement detailing future career goals and how this scholarship will help achieve them.
4. Include a certified copy of the latest academic transcript.

## Deadline
May 1st of every calendar year.
